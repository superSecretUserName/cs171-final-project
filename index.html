Eye to the Sky - Chandra Edition

Rotate the globe to display the Chandra sky as viewed from the target point on the Earth. Each yellow dot in the view below represents a target observed by the X-ray space telescope Chandra. You can hover over points in the sky to view more information about the object observed and observation scientific goals.

Clicking on a target will change the cycle view bubble chart in the right area. If you hover over a bubble in the right area, more detailed information will be shown about that object. The bubble chart shows every proposal approved for observation each year in Chandra's 15 year lifetime.
